To measure the success of the transition system:

1.  **Average Turnaround Time (TAT)**: Time from request submission to final closure. (Target: < 3 days for Offboarding).
2.  **First-Time Approval Rate**: Percentage of requests approved without needing corrections.
3.  **Asset Recovery Rate**: % of company assets (laptops, phones) successfully returned within 24 hours of LWD.
4.  **System Adoption**: % of transitions processed via the system vs. email/paper.
